How I work

How I work

Scaling software companies reach a point where the founding team can no longer hold everything together by instinct. Delivery slips, communication weakens, and what gets built stops being what was meant.

I work with companies at exactly that moment, typically between 10 and 50 people.

Step 1

Embedded Delivery Reset

A fixed-scope, fixed-deliverable engagement. I embed for 4 to 8 weeks, producing a clear diagnosis and a concrete implementation plan. You leave with clarity on what needs to change and a plan to make it happen.

Step 2

Fractional Delivery Leadership

I stay on as your fractional delivery leader, owning execution of the plan through to a defined milestone.

Get in touch to talk it through, or start with the free diagnostic.
